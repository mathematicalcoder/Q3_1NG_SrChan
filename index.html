Generating Functions

We define a generating function of the sequence \(a_0,a_1,a_2,\dots\) as the polynomial $$\small P(x)=a_0+a_1x+a_2x^2+a_3x^3+\dots.$$ This video by 3Blue1Brown shows how generating functions can be applied to discrete math and counting problems.
